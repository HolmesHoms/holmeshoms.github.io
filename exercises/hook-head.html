Welcome to Hook Head Lighthouse

Your Gateway to Coastal Beauty and History

Explore the Historic Lighthouse

Step back in time and visit one of the oldest operational lighthouses in the world. With its rich history dating back to the 12th century, Hook Head Lighthouse is a must-see attraction.

Learn about the fascinating role this iconic structure has played in maritime safety over the centuries during our guided tours.

Enjoy Scenic Coastal Views

Take in breathtaking panoramic views of the rugged coastline and the Atlantic Ocean. Perfect for photography enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Relax at our nearby picnic area and enjoy the peaceful sound of the waves crashing against the rocks.
